Crispy Baked Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds chicken wings
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons ground paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a shallow baking dish with aluminum foil and grease lightly.
  2. Cut tips from chicken wings and discard. Cut between the joint to
    separate each wing into 2 pieces.
  3. Combine butter and garlic in a small saucepan over low heat; heat until butter is melted
    and garlic is fragrant, 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Mix Parmesan cheese, parsley, oregano, paprika, salt, and pepper together in a shallow bowl.
