Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon water (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon margarine or butter
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a cup or small bowl, whisk together the eggs, mayonnaise and water using a fork. Melt margarine in a skillet over low heat.
    Pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook.
    Remove the eggs to a plate when they are set, but still moist. Do not over cook. Never add salt or pepper until eggs are on plate,
    but these are also good without.
